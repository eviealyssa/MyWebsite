Different colours bring out different eyes. To achieve the best eye look, know what colours work for you.



Blue Eyes

Warm, earthy tones make blue eyes pop. By layering light shades of neutral browns, orange and peach is the perfect backdrop to your blue eyes as it makes them pop. By day, choose matte colours for a subtler enhancement and by night add a little more drama by going for shimmery golds and bronze.

A good palette for blue eyes is NYX Professional Palette in warm neutral

Brown Eyes

For best results, cool blue, plum and green tones tend to do a great job of balancing out the warmth of brown eyes and are completely wearable during the day. But, if using brighter colours isn’t your style then neutral shades can also work wonders. For a lift, add violet or silver to really give your eyes a boost

A good palette for brown eyes is Wet N Wild icon 10 Palette

Green Eyes

As green eyes are naturally bright, you may find you don’t need to contrast them too heavily with vibrant colours, go for soft palettes of shimmery brown, charcoal grey and forest green eyeshadows. If you’re feeling adventurous, you can even experiment with mixing some of these colours together to create a slightly richer finish. A swipe of gold shadow can bring out any flecks of yellow, giving a brighter look.

A good palette for blue eyes is 15N Night Mastery Palette
