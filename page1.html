Foundation is your makeup support system - it helps neautralise skin tones aswell as minimise the apperance of pores. Here our experts have condensed all the best tips so you can have the flawless skin that you deserve.

  • The correct shade is key - find your skin's undertone before choosing a foundation (you won't want to look like an oompa loompa!)
  • When selecting a foundation, choose one which suits your skin type for example, if you have oily skin choose a matte product
  • When it comes to applying foundation, there are many options to choose from - it is up to your peronal preferance to select whether you want to use a beauty blender, a brush or even your own hands!
